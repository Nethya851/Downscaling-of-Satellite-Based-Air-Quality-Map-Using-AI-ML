About the Project

Satellites like Sentinel-5P give us wide-area pollutant readings, but at a coarse resolution. This project combines satellite pollutant columns (NO2, SO2, CO, O3, AOD), local weather conditions, and CPCB/TNPCB ground station AQI readings to train an AI model that downscales coarse satellite data into a high-resolution AQI map — giving accurate, location-specific air quality predictions anywhere in Tamil Nadu, even where no ground station exists.

# 1. Project Title

**Downscaling of Satellite-Based Air Quality Map Using AI/ML**

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Satellite-based air quality maps provide valuable information about pollution levels over large regions. However, these maps are often available at a coarse spatial resolution, making it difficult to analyze air quality at local and city levels. There is a need for a system that can enhance the resolution of satellite-derived air quality data to provide more accurate and detailed pollution information for environmental monitoring and decision-making.

---

# 3. Project Objectives

* To collect and process satellite-based air quality data.
* To develop an AI/ML model for downscaling low-resolution air quality maps.
* To generate high-resolution air quality maps with improved spatial detail.
* To analyze local pollution patterns more accurately.
* To support environmental monitoring, urban planning, and public health initiatives.

---

# 4. Module List

### Module 1: Data Collection

Collect satellite air quality data and relevant environmental datasets.

### Module 2: Data Preprocessing

Clean, normalize, and prepare the data for machine learning.

### Module 3: Feature Engineering

Extract and select important features affecting air quality.

### Module 4: AI/ML Model Development

Train and evaluate machine learning models for map downscaling.

### Module 5: High-Resolution Map Generation

Generate detailed air quality maps from low-resolution satellite data.

### Module 6: Visualization and Analysis

Visualize air quality patterns and analyze model performance.
